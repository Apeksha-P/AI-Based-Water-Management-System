AI-Based Water Management System

Our AI-based water management system is a comprehensive solution designed to address the diverse needs of water resource monitoring and management within an academic institution. The system ensures secure access through user authentication, allowing only individuals with a kln.ac.lk email extension to log in, thus maintaining institutional security. With role-based access control, students, staff, and administrators can navigate the system according to their permissions, with administrators having enhanced capabilities for managing user accounts.

The Dashboard provides real-time data visualization, enabling users to monitor water usage, temperature, pH levels, and Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) through intuitive graphical representations.

This feature allows for immediate insights into water quality and consumption patterns, helping users make informed decisions. Additionally, the Analysis page offers the ability to review and filter historical data over various time frames, facilitating trend analysis and supporting long-term planning.

Our system also integrates predictive analytics to forecast future water usage and estimate upcoming water bills based on historical data. This AI-driven feature is instrumental in proactive resource planning and budgeting. Administrators have the ability to input daily meter readings, which are automatically updated within the system, ensuring data accuracy and up-to-date metrics.

To cater to the individual needs of users, the system includes a profile management feature, allowing students, staff, and administrators to update their personal information easily.
